“Think in colour.” Hillary

Born and raised in southern California, I grew up surrounded by surf culture. Turning white surfboards into colorful, energy filled canvases has become my passion.

FEATURED ARTIST: Hillary Dunks

Graffiti street art has been a big inspiration for many of my designs, as well as elements found in our natural world. I have had the opportunity to paint several boards for pro surfer Lakey Peterson, including the "Pray for Daisy" series ridden at the 2012 Nike US Open of Surfing in Huntington Beach, Ca.
